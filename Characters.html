PARKER WILSON

A kind, responsible and sensitive university professor. Upon encountering the dog Hachiko at a train station, Parker develops a deep bond of friendship and loyalty with him. He is a family man and animal lover, whose daily routine reinforces the special bond with Hachiko. His unconditional love and relationship with the dog become the central axis of the story, symbolizing the devotion and bond between humans and animals.

Characters
